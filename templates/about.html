What is Pocket Bookcase for?

Do you often buy a book and then realise you've already read it or you already have it on your bookshelf?

OR are you just generally a book fiend and can't keep track of where your are in the numerous book series you have started?

Then Pocket Bookcase is here to help you keep track.

How do I use it?

You can use the Library to find your next book to read by using the search bar.

You can sign-up which allows you to add books from the library to your profile.

If a book isn't in our library you can add it yourself.

You can edit books you have added to the library.

You can also edit or delete books that you have on your profile.

How do I sign up?

You can sign-up by clicking on the Register link in the navigation menu or the footer. Its free to use and you can delete your account at any time, via the edit account link in the footer.

I'm having trouble logging in!

Contact us via the menu link and our admin will help you out.
